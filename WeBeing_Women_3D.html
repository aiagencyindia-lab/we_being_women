Our Story

Born from a
woman's dream

We Being Women began as a simple belief — that the hands of Indian women hold centuries of wisdom, artistry, and grace that the world deserves to know.

From the jewellers of Rajasthan to the weavers of Bengal, from Ayurvedic herbalists to block-print artists — every woman behind this platform is a founder in her own right.

We don't just sell products. We celebrate the women who make them.
